What is a webleaf,

and how do I make one?

workshop this thursday, oct 30

you're hereby invited to participate in the workshop hosted by me as part of the ghostlife project. After a short introduction to the concept of webleaves and whorls as a possible modes of personal publishing, each person will create one with simple and free tools.

It will take place at my studio in the akbild sculpture building:

Kurzbauergasse 9, room 1.12 from 6.30pm to ~9pm

You walk into the main entrance, go left and follow the corridor.
Then ascend to the first floor and walk along the corridor until you see a large toolbox.

please bring the following:

1. your laptop
2. warm clothes (the heating system of the building is in repair)
3. something you'd like to share with the internet (no more than 3mb)

This is the very first workshop I'm hosting and I'm no expert at anything, so expect the unexpected... I'll provide some snacks and tea. See you on thursday! :)
